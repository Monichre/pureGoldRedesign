Jud Nichols

Jud Nichols, Founder of Pure Gold, is a video producer and lawyer who found his passion for cameras while stranded on a deserted island filming a reality TV show called The Island.

Jud's interests are both technical and creative. Combining his artistic eye with his fluency in video editing, he's filmed a variety of content from documentaries in Nebraska to twerking parties on rooftops of Minneapolis. He will travel any distance to find the most compelling stories. That's what Pure Gold is all about.

2016 / Pure Gold / About

Pure Gold is a Minneapolis based Creative Film Agency focused on stunning visual storytelling. Our passion for the narrative within us all; this is the cornerstone of our company. We also like jokes. and gadgets. and design.

We create quick, fresh and relevant digital media content for the web and beyond (is there much beyond?). From weddings and industrials to comedy shorts and promotionals, we do it all. With stylish cuts, stunning frames and aerial technology, Pure Gold is the right move for your vision. So go ahead and live in the moment, we'll make it last forever.
